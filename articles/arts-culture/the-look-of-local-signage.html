The Look of Local Signage

How storefront typography quietly shapes the visual culture of the Tri-Cities.

March 2026
By Civilization Tri-Cities
Image Placeholder

Drive through almost any commercial corridor in the Tri-Cities and a strange visual pattern begins to emerge. Fonts repeat themselves. Colors echo from one storefront to another. Neon signs glow over strip mall parking lots. The result is a kind of accidental regional typography.
