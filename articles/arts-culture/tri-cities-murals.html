Arts + Culture

Tri-Cities Murals

Murals in the Tri-Cities add color and identity to spaces that might otherwise feel purely functional. They can turn a blank wall into a landmark and help distinguish one district from another.

In a region shaped by infrastructure, agriculture, and suburban growth, murals also introduce local texture. They suggest that culture is not separate from the built environment, but part of how a place becomes recognizable to itself.
