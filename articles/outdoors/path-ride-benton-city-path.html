Path Ride: Benton City Path

A short GoPro ride along a local path near Benton City.

By Civilization Tri-Cities

The Benton City Path offers a simple local ride with open views, small-town edges, and a slower look at the landscape west of the Tri-Cities.

This short ride video is part of the Path Ride series, a recurring look at bike paths, riverfront routes, neighborhood connectors, and easy regional rides within reach of the Tri-Cities.
