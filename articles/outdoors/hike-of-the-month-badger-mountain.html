Hike of the Month: Badger Mountain

A familiar local climb with expansive views across the Tri-Cities and the surrounding Columbia Basin.

By Civilization Tri-Cities

March 2026

Photo Placeholder

This month’s featured hike is Badger Mountain, one of the most recognizable trail destinations in the Tri-Cities. Rising above the south side of Richland, the mountain offers a straightforward climb and a broad vantage point over the basin below.

The trail begins near the Badger Mountain trailhead in Richland and follows a steady path up the hillside to the summit ridge. The route is moderately challenging but accessible for many hikers at a comfortable pace. Because the trail is largely exposed, bringing water and planning for sun exposure is recommended, particularly during warmer months.

From the top, hikers can see across the Tri-Cities, the Columbia River corridor, and the surrounding ridgelines that frame the basin. On clear days the view stretches far beyond the cities themselves, revealing the layered meeting of shrub-steppe landscape, irrigated farmland, and expanding suburban neighborhoods that define this region.
