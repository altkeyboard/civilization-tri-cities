About Civilization Tri-Cities

Civilization Tri-Cities is an independent regional publication focused on home, design, arts, culture, and the outdoor landscape of the Tri-Cities region.

It is interested in how people shape the Columbia Basin through housing, infrastructure, irrigation, planning, visual culture, and everyday land use.
